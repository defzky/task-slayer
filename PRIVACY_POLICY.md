# Privacy Policy for Task Slayer

**Last updated: January 27, 2026**

Task Slayer ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Chrome Extension collects, uses, and safeguards your information.

## 1. Data Collection and Usage

Task Slayer is designed to prioritize user privacy. We comply with the Chrome Web Store Developer Program Policies regarding data usage.

### A. Web History and Website Content
**Feature:** Context Freezer (Stasis)
*   **What we access:** When you choose to "Freeze" a context, the extension reads the **URL** and **Title** of the tabs currently open in your active window.
*   **Why we access it:** This data is required to save your browsing session so it can be restored later.
*   **Where it is stored:** This data is stored strictly **locally** on your device (via `chrome.storage.local`) or synced to your own Google Account (via `chrome.storage.sync`) if Cloud Sync is enabled.
*   **We DO NOT** transmit this data to any external servers, analytics platforms, or third parties.

### B. User Activity and Progress
**Feature:** RPG Quest Board & Shop
*   **What we access:** We store your created quests, notes, XP, level, and purchased items.
*   **Where it is stored:** Stored locally on your device or synced via your personal Google Account.

## 2. Data Security

*   **No Remote Payloads:** All code is bundled within the extension. We do not execute remote code.
*   **Local Processing:** All logic regarding experience points, gold, and task completion happens locally in your browser.

## 3. Third-Party Sharing

We **DO NOT** sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information or browsing data to outside parties.

## 4. User Consent

By installing and using Task Slayer, you consent to the processing of your data as described in this policy.

## 5. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, you may contact us via the Chrome Web Store support tab.
